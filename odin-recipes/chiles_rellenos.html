Chiles Rellenos

Description

Chiles Rellenos are traditional Mexican stuffed poblano peppers, typically filled with cheese or meat, dipped in an egg batter, and fried to perfection. A delightful dish perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 6 large poblano peppers
  • 1 cup shredded cheese (or cooked meat of your choice)
  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: Tomato sauce for serving

Steps

  • Step 1: Roast the poblano peppers over an open flame or under a broiler until the skin is charred. Place them in a plastic bag for 10 minutes to steam, then peel off the skin. Carefully make a slit in each pepper and remove the seeds.
  • Step 2: Stuff each pepper with cheese or your preferred filling and close the slit with a toothpick if needed.
  • Step 3: Beat the egg whites in a bowl until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in the yolks and season with salt.
  • Step 4: Lightly coat each stuffed pepper with flour and then dip it into the egg mixture, ensuring it's fully covered.
  • Step 5: Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Fry each pepper until golden brown on all sides, then drain on paper towels.
  • Step 6: Serve hot with tomato sauce or enjoy as is!
